FAMS

Fly Ash Management System.

Fly Ash Management System is a state-of-the-art solution to manage the FLY ASH generated while generating power at thermal power plants. The system is tightly integrated with silo sensors / weigh bridges and payment gateways.

The system provides online information on the fly ash available to the registered users. All the contracts signed by client for lifting the fly ash are digitally stored and based on the agreed quantities allocation happens on daily basis.

The said software has full workflow automated from the Forecast, request, Allocate and invoice.

ITMS

Intlligent Traffic Management System

The traffic management system lacks in Intelligent Traffic Control System, traffic management plans for individual signal controls and groups of signal controllers along with pre planned intervention strategies for special scenarios ae required to plan for well managed traffic system.

Out ITMS Intelligent Traffic Management System with following critical modules help city authorities to manage city traffic with better control and ease.



01. Entry Exit Point Management with Smart Hybrid Traffic sensors along with ANPR for all major Entry Exit points to have precise information about vehicle entering, exiting, monitoring and control of traffic in primary city corridor for Traffic Information, management Control

02. Corridor Management with Variable Messaging sign Boards, speed sensors and Camera's.

03. Traffic Enforcement System RLVD, SVD

04.Adaptive Traffic Control System - Adaptive Signal controller with safety integration level certificate, Traffic light aspects, poles, power supply provisioning and related accessories and associated civil work including cabling for successful operation of the system.
